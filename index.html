A Student Biography

"The Continuing Lifelong Journey of an Artist turned Graphics Designer then Back to School Once More"

by Mike Yencha

My Name is Mike Yencha. I'm a classically trained artist who started doing code almost by accident. Working for a company who needed marketing design support, I informed them I would like to learn code. I thought I would get direct training before starting, however, they had different ideas. One morning when I walked into work I was informed that in a week I'd be sitting on-site. It was a sink or swim moment and with no formal training, I turned to the Internet.

That week I went to Lynda.com and began taking HTML and CSS classes. I give James Williamson complete credit for giving me the basics I needed to get started. I must also thank Charlene Slayton for teaching me the practicalities of surviving the development environment. I'm a graphics designer who has been doing HTML and CSS for a large government project for 5 years. But I'm interested in learning more and expading my horizons into development.
